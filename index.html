// WHO USES IT?
🧠 Psychiatrists — used as a baseline before prescribing antidepressants and to track treatment response over time.
🛋 Therapists & Counselors — administered at the start of CBT sessions to monitor symptom change week-by-week.
🏥 Hospitals & Clinics — used in emergency departments and inpatient wards to screen for depression severity.
🔬 Researchers — the gold-standard outcome measure in thousands of depression, anxiety, and medication trials worldwide.
📱 Digital Health Apps — integrated into mental health platforms like BetterHelp, Woebot, and NHS digital pathways.
// HOW SCORING WORKS

Each of the 21 questions covers a different symptom of depression. You pick the statement that best describes how you've been feeling over the past two weeks. Each answer is scored 0 (not at all) to 3 (severe).

01–10 Normal ups & downs
11–16 Mild mood disturbance
17–20 Borderline clinical depression
21–30 Moderate depression
31–40 Severe depression
40–63 Extreme depression
